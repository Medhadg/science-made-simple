About

Science Made Simple (SMS) is a clarity-driven platform translating complex scientific systems into actionable understanding.

Founded by Dr. Medhanjali Dasgupta, SMS integrates structural biology, AI-driven decision systems, and science storytelling to reduce bottlenecks in research and innovation.

What I Build

  • Decision logic frameworks for structural biology workflows
  • AI-guided experimental bottleneck mapping
  • Scientific storytelling platforms (Mila, zines, visual systems)
  • Human-centered research system design

Who It’s For

  • Academic labs navigating experimental complexity
  • Biotech & AI startups optimizing structure-to-property workflows
  • Foundations and nonprofits translating science for public engagement

Status

SMS is currently in development. Select collaborations and consulting inquiries are welcome.
