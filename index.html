About

Science Made Simple (SMS) is a clarity-driven platform translating bench reality into AI-ready structures so models and experiments stay aligned.

Founded by Dr. Medhanjali Dasgupta, SMS integrates structural biology with AI-driven decision systems to reduce bottlenecks in research innovation.

What I Build

  • Decision logic frameworks for structural biology workflows
  • AI-guided experimental bottleneck mapping
  • Scientific storytelling platforms (Mila, zines, visual systems)
  • Human-centered research system design

Who It’s For

  • Academic labs navigating experimental complexity
  • Biotech & AI startups optimizing structure-to-property workflows
  • Foundations and nonprofits translating science for public engagement and education
