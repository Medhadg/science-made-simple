# Science Made Simple (SMS)

**Turning experimental uncertainty into structured decisions.**

Science Made Simple builds decision frameworks for structural biology and experimental research teams navigating complex iteration loops.

I help labs and biotech groups identify bottlenecks, map failure modes, and choose the next best experiment with clearer signal and less waste. Where useful, I translate bench reality into AI-ready structure so models and experiments stay aligned.

---

## Problem

Modern experimental workflows are increasingly complex:

- Structural data is abundant but noisy  
- AI models are powerful but often disconnected from wet-lab constraints  
- Iteration cycles are slow and decision logic is rarely explicit  

The primary bottleneck is often not technical capability — it is unclear decision structure.

---

## Approach

SMS develops:

- Experiment-to-decision logic frameworks  
- Failure-mode maps  
- Bottleneck diagnostics  
- AI–wet lab alignment systems
- Simplified science for education and outreach 

The goal: make scientific reasoning explicit, structured, reproducible and understandable to diverse audiences.
